Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

I design and build modern, high-performing websites using Vue.js, tailored to your business goals, with options to integrate AI systems for automation and scalability.

Who do you work with?

I partner with medium-sized businesses and growth-focused companies seeking to enhance their online presence, streamline operations, and drive measurable growth.

What makes your websites different?

Unlike generic templates, I create custom-built, scalable solutions with smooth animations (GSAP, Lottie, Locomotive Scroll) and future-ready technology. My sites are optimized for performance, SEO, and user experience, ensuring they not only look great but also drive results.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes. I offer training, maintenance packages, and optimization plans to keep your site secure, fast, and aligned with evolving business needs.

How long does a project take?

Typical websites are delivered in 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. Larger projects with AI integration may require longer timelines.
